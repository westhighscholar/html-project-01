Interesting Facts

  • Bulldogs are peaceful and even-tempered. They are not mean or vicious and they are not aggressive. They usually don't pick fights. They are not easily disturbed or upset. Bulldogs make great family pets.
  • Bulldogs are medium size. They look small, but probably weigh more than you think. Males weigh about 50 pounds and females tend to be a little smaller, weighing about 40 pounds. Both dogs can be from 12 - 16 inches tall.
  • A Bulldog's coat is short and lies flat. The hair is fine and very smooth. A healthy Bulldog's coat is shiny. Bulldogs have a short tail. Bulldog pups and adults have soft, loose skin around their head, neck, and shoulders. Even pups have lots of wrinkles.
